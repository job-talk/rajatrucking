Services We Provide

Raja Trucking, LLC is committed to provide you with excellent service with on time deliveries and safety of your commodities as our number one priority. With Raja Trucking, LLC, you can rest assured that your product is in safe hands. Raja Trucking, LLC offers transportation of your freight throughout the 48 states.

We provide the following services:

  • 24 hour a day dispatching service
  • Hot Shot Load

  • Local / Regional / OTR Transportation Service

Raja Trucking, LLC offers transportation services of dry cargo and drayage. We, at Raja Trucking, LLC, commit to safely deliver your products and deliver you exemplary service. Our drivers have gone through an intense training, background verification and have several years of experience. We uphold a strong code of ethics providing a personal commitment to deliver on time and that your shipment is in outstanding condition from start to finish!
