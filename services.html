Services We Provide

Raja Trucking, LLC is committed to provide you with excellent service with on time deliveries and safety of your commodities as our number one priority. With Raja Trucking LLC, you can rest assure that your product is in safe hands. Your equipment and merchandise will be insured with up to two million ($2,000,000) in aggregate general liability and two hundred and fifty thousand ($250,000) in cargo liability. Raja Trucking LLC offers transportation of your freight within Texas, Oklahoma, Arkansas, Kansas and Louisiana.

We provide the following services:

  • 24 hour a day dispatching service
  • Hot Shot Load
  • Full Truck Load (FTL) & Less then Truck Load (LTL) service
  • Local & Regional Transportation Service
  • Container storage rental facility

Raja Trucking, LLC. offers transportation services of dry cargo and drayage. We, at Raja Trucking, LLC, commit to safely deliver your products and deliver you exemplary service. Our drivers have gone through an intense training, background verification and have several years of experience. We uphold a strong code of ethics providing a personal commitment to deliver on time and that your shipment is in outstanding condition from start to finish!

To obtain more information on our services feel free to call or email us. You can find this information under the Contact Us portion of the website.
