Welcome to Raja Trucking, LLC.

Raja Trucking, LLC. is a uprising freight transportation company that provide services for freight brokers and various company to locations in Texas, Oklahoma, Kansas, Arkansas, and Louisiana. We have been in business in the Fort Worth, TX area since May 2006. In the past year, Raja Trucking, LLC. has grown significantly having only six contract drivers to 13 contract drivers in less than one year of inception.
