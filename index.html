Welcome to Raja Trucking, LLC.

Raja Trucking, LLC. is an uprising freight transportation company that provide services for freight brokers and various companies to locations throughout the 48 states. We have been in business in the Fort Worth, TX area since May 2006. Raja Trucking, LLC. is operating with about 30 drivers providing power only, intermodal, and over the road (OTR) services. For more information regarding our services, please feel free to contact us.
